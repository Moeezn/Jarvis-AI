1. **Inside Out (2015)**: This Pixar film tells the story of a young girl named Riley and her emotions as she navigates a big change in her life.
2. **Zootopia (2016)**: This Disney film is set in a world where animals have evolved to live in harmony with each other and follows the story of a rabbit police officer and a fox con artist as they solve a mystery.
3. **Moana (2016)**: This Disney film tells the story of a young Polynesian princess who sets sail on a daring mission to save her island and restore the heart of the goddess Te Fiti.
4. **Kubo and the Two Strings (2016)**: This stop-motion film tells the story of a young boy named Kubo who must use his magical abilities to defeat an evil spirit and save his family.

8. **Frozen (2013)**: This Disney film tells the story of two sisters, Elsa and Anna, as they navigate their complicated relationship and the secrets of their past.
9. **The Incredibles (2004)**: This Pixar film tells the story of a family with superpowers as they try to live a normal life but are forced back into action to save the world from a villainous plot.
10. **Spirited Away (2001)**: This Studio Ghibli film tells the story of a young girl named Chihiro who is transported to a magical world where she must work in a bathhouse for spirits to find her way home.
