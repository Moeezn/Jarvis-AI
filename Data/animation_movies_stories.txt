**Animation Movie Stories**

Here are some popular animation movie stories:

**1. Toy Story (1995)**

In a world where toys come to life when humans are not around, a toy cowboy named Woody is obsessed with being played with by his owner, Andy. However, when a new toy, Buzz Lightyear, enters the picture, Woody becomes jealous and feels threatened. The two toys must put aside their differences and work together to get back to their owner before they are left behind.

**2. The Lion King (1994)**

In the Pride Lands of Africa, a young lion named Simba is born to King Mufasa and Queen Sarabi. After Mufasa's death, Simba's evil uncle Scar takes over the throne and convinces Simba that he is responsible for Mufasa's death. Simba runs away from home and grows up with a meerkat and warthog. Years later, Simba's childhood friend Nala finds him and tells him about the suffering of the Pride Lands under Scar's rule. Simba must face his destiny and reclaim his rightful place as king.

**3. Finding Nemo (2003)**

Marlin, a clownfish, lives in the Great Barrier Reef with his son Nemo. Nemo is captured by a diver and taken to a fish tank in a dentist's office. Desperate to rescue his son, Marlin sets out on an epic journey to find Nemo. Along the way, he meets a forgetful fish named Dory, who joins him on his quest. Together, they face many dangers and obstacles, but ultimately, they are able to rescue Nemo and return him to his father.

**4. Frozen (2013)**

In the kingdom of Arendelle, Princess Elsa has the power to create and control ice and snow. However, she has been keeping her powers a secret, afraid of hurting those she loves. On the day of her coronation as queen, Elsa's powers are revealed, and she accidentally plunges the kingdom into eternal winter. Her sister, Anna, sets out on a journey to find Elsa and convince her to return and reverse the curse. Along the way, Anna meets Kristoff, an ice harvester, and his reindeer Sven, and together they face many challenges to save the kingdom.

**5. The Jungle Book (2016)**

Mowgli, a human boy, is raised by wolves in the jungle. When the tiger Shere Khan, who killed Mowgli's parents, returns to the jungle, Mowgli must leave the only home he has ever known. With the help of a bear named Baloo and a panther named Bagheera, Mowgli navigates the dangers of the jungle and learns to control his emotions and trust himself. Along the way, he must face Shere Khan and his minions, but ultimately, he is able to defeat them and find his place in the jungle.

**6. Zootopia (2016)**

In the city of Zootopia, a rabbit named Judy Hopps becomes the first rabbit police officer. She teams up with a fox named Nick Wilde to solve a mystery involving the disappearance of several animals. As they delve deeper into the case, they uncover a conspiracy involving the city's mayor and a sinister plot to exploit the city's predators. Judy and Nick must work together to solve the case and prove that anyone can be anything they want to be, regardless of their species or background.

**7. Moana (2016)**

Moana, a young Polynesian princess, is chosen by the ocean to restore the heart of the goddess Te Fiti. According to legend, Te Fiti's heart was stolen by the demigod Maui, causing a curse that slowly destroys the islands and the ocean. Moana sets sail on a daring mission to find Maui, force him to return Te Fiti's heart, and save her island and the ocean. Along the way, she discovers her own identity and the secrets of her people's past.

**8. The Incredibles (2004)**

Bob Parr, a former superhero known as Mr. Incredible, is forced to hide his powers and live a normal life with his wife Helen and their children, Dash and Violet. However, when a villainous plot threatens the city, Bob is forced back into action, and his family must join him to save the day. As they work together to stop the villain, they must also confront their own personal struggles and learn to work together as a family.

**9. Shrek (2001)**

In a swampy kingdom, an ogre named Shrek lives a solitary life, feared and avoided by the people of the nearby kingdom. However, when his swamp is invaded by fairy tale creatures, Shrek makes a deal with the kingdom's ruler, Lord Farquaad, to rescue a princess from a dragon-guarded tower in exchange for the return of his swamp. Along the way, Shrek meets a talking donkey named Donkey and the princess, Fiona, who is not what she seems.

**10. Monsters, Inc. (2001)**

In a world where monsters live in their own society, two monsters, Mike and Sulley, work at a company that generates electricity by scaring children. However, when a little girl, Boo, accidentally follows Sulley back into the monster world, Mike and Sulley must return her to her own world before she is discovered. Along the way, they learn that laughter is a more powerful energy source than fear and that children are not as scary as they thought.
